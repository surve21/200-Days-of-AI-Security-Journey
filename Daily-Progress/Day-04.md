 Key Takeaways
Firewalls are the first line of defense in network security.
VPNs provide secure communication across untrusted networks.
Proxy servers improve security, privacy, and traffic management.
Wireless and IoT environments require additional security controls.
Static Routing is suitable for small and predictable network environments.
